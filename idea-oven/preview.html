How to snap your fingers

A real skill. Useful for committing.

1Position
Press the pad of your thumb against the pad of your middle finger. Index and ring stay relaxed.
2Pressure
Push the two pads hard against each other.
3Release
Slide the middle finger off the thumb fast. The middle finger slams down into the base of your thumb. That hit is the snap.
